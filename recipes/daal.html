Masoor Daal

Description

This is a home made recipe that can be made with many common ingredients from home. Any type of daal (this recipe is for masoor daal), some basic ingredients, and a kitchen is all you need to get started!

Ingredients

  • Masoor Daal
  • Cumin and Coriander Powder
  • Garlic and Ginger Paste
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Mustard Powder
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Chilli Pepper
  • Lemon Juice
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill a bowl half way with daal and wash it
  2. Add 4 cups of water into pot
  3. Add the following into the pot and stir well
    • 1.5 spoon cumin powder
    • 1.5 spoon salt
    • 1.5 spoon red chilli powder
    • >.5 spoon mustard powder
    • 1.5 table spoon garlic and ginger paste
    • 1-2 sliced tomatoes
    • 1 sliced green chhilli pepper
  5. Let this mixture boil for 25 minutes then add 2 table spoon lemon juice
  6. For tharka cut >.5 onion and .25 butter stick and stir in pan (some chilli if you want)
  7. Once onions are slighly brown add this mixture to pot with daal
