Boneless Chicekn Pasta

Description

This is a home made recipe that can be made with many common ingredients from home. Any type of boneless chicken, any kind of pasta, and a kitchen is all you need to get started!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Pasta
  • Ginger Paste
  • Coriander Powder
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Mustard Powder
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken
    1. Slice the chicken breasts to ideal size
    2. Grab a bowl
      • Add 1.5 table spoon ginger paste
      • Add a splash of cooking oil
      • Add .5 table spoon of coriander powder
      • Add .5 table spoon of red chilli powder
      • Add a pinch of mustard powder
    3. Stir the bowl well to create the seasoning
    4. Add the sliced chicken breasts into the bowl and stir well
    5. Add 1 cup water into bowl and set it on the stove to boil and eventually water will evaporate
  2. Prepare the Pasta
    1. Fill an appropriate sized pot with water (should be about 3/4 of the pot)
    2. Add 1 tea spoon of salt
    3. Add 1 table sppon of cookin oil
    4. Once the water is boiling add the pasta into pot
    5. Let it cook for 10-15 minutes
    6. If the pasta is tender it is ready, if not wait few a more minutes and check again
    7. Drain the pasta
  3. Mix the pasta and chicken and stir
  4. Let it cook for a few minutes and check to see if it is done
