Masoor Daal

Description

This is a home made recipe that can be made with many common ingredients from home. Some basic ingredients and a kitchen is all you need to get started!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Cumin and Coriander Powder
  • Garlic and Ginger Paste
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Mustard Powder
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Chilli Pepper
  • Paprika Powder
  • Onion
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Cut onions and put into pan and let it warm up
  2. Add .5 cup of cooking oil as it is warming up
  3. Let this steam until onions are slightly brown
  4. Add some water and let it steam for 5 minutes with lid on
  5. While waiting cut the tomatoes and green chilli pepper
  6. Add the sliced tomatoes and pepper with the masala into pan after 5 minutes are up
  7. Mash the tomatoes while they are warming up in pan every couple minutes
  8. Add the sliced potatoes into pan after tomatoes are mashed and slightly cooked
  9. Mix them well and check every 5 minutes until potatoes are tender
  10. Once potatoes are tender add the eggs into pan and mix well (don't let eggs stick to pan)
  11. Add green chilli pepper, cumin seeds, and coriander leaves adn let it steam for 5 minutes
  12. Check if done if everything is cooked well
